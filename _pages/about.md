<div class="home-intro">
  <p class="home-intro__text">I'm a Ph.D. student at the <a href="https://cix.cs.uni-saarland.de/">Computational Interaction Lab</a>, Saarland University. My research focuses on predicting where users look when interacting with interfaces — and what that reveals about the future of Human–AI collaboration. I also have prior experience in human reliability analysis and data visualization.</p>
